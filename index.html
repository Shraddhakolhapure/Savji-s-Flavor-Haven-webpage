About Us

Shree Savji Khanaval was started by a couple, Rupa and Laxmikant, who had a passion for cooking and wanted to create a place where people could enjoy homely, delicious food. They started this humble khanavali, offering mouthwatering non-veg dishes like Anda Curry, Chicken Curry, and Mutton Curry. Over the years, their commitment to quality and taste has made Shree Savji Khanaval a household name in Solapur.
